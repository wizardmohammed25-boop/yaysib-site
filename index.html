Diploma in Computer Application with internet.

Our Diploma in Computer Application with internet is perfect for beginners looking to build a solid foundation in basic computer operations. Students will learn essential computer skills such as:

  • Microsoft Office Applications: Master MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint..
  • Internet Basics: Learn to navigate the web efficiently and use email services.
  • File Management: Understand how to manage digital files and folders.

This course is ideal for those looking to improve their productivity or start a career in office administration.

Diploma in Computer Networking

Our Diploma in Computer Networking equips students with the skills to design, implement, and manage network systems. The curriculum covers:

  • Networking Essentials: Explore network topologies, protocols, and devices.
  • Network Configuration: Learn how to configure routers, switches, and firewalls.
  • Troubleshooting: Gain expertise in identifying and resolving network issues.

This program is tailored for individuals aspiring to become network administrators or IT support professionals.

Diploma in Computer Engineering

The Diploma in Computer Engineering focuses on hardware and system-level knowledge. Students will explore:

  • Hardware Components: Learn how to assemble, troubleshoot, and repair computers.
  • Operating Systems: Study system installation, maintenance, and optimization.
  • Hardware-Software Integration: Understand the interaction between hardware and software systems.

This course is suited for students aiming to pursue careers in hardware engineering or IT support.

Diploma in Programming

The Diploma in Programming provides hands-on experience in coding and software development. Students will work with:

  • Programming Languages: Learn key languages like Python, Java, PHP, and C++.
  • Web Development: Master front-end and back-end development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.
  • Software Engineering Principles: Apply best practices in software design and development.

Ideal for aspiring software developers and programmers.

Diploma in Graphics Design

The Diploma in Graphics Design nurtures creativity and technical skills to produce stunning visual content. Students will learn:

  • Design Principles: Understand layout, typography, and color theory.
  • Software Tools: Master Adobe Photoshop, CorelDRAW, and Canva.
  • Project-Based Learning: Create logos, posters, brochures, and social media content.

This course is ideal for future graphic designers, marketers, and content creators.

Diploma in Data Science

Our Diploma in Data Science offers in-depth knowledge in data analysis and management. The program covers:

  • Data Analytics Tools: Learn to use Python, R, and Excel for data analysis.
  • Database Management: Explore SQL and database optimization techniques.
  • Data Visualization: Gain expertise in presenting data insights using visualization tools.

Perfect for students interested in pursuing careers in data analysis, business intelligence, or research.

Diploma in Cyber Security

Our Diploma in Cyber Security equips students with the skills to protect digital systems and data from threats. The program covers:

  • Network Security: Understand how to secure network infrastructure against attacks and vulnerabilities.
  • Ethical Hacking: Learn penetration testing techniques and how to identify security loopholes.
  • Cyber Laws & Policies: Explore legal and regulatory frameworks governing cybersecurity.
  • Malware Analysis: Get introduced to analyzing and defending against malicious software.

Ideal for students aiming for careers in information security, ethical hacking, and cyber threat analysis.

Diploma in Cloud Computing

Our Diploma in Cloud Computing provides comprehensive training in cloud technologies, services, and infrastructure. The program covers:

  • Cloud Platforms: Learn to work with major cloud providers like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud.
  • Virtualization & Containers: Understand how to deploy and manage virtual machines and containers using tools like Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Cloud Security & Compliance: Explore best practices for securing cloud environments and meeting regulatory standards.
  • DevOps & Automation: Get hands-on with cloud deployment pipelines and automation tools for continuous integration and delivery (CI/CD).

Ideal for students interested in careers as cloud engineers, DevOps professionals, and infrastructure specialists.

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer at Yaysib Computer?

We offer a wide range of IT courses, including Computer Appreciation, Networking, Programming (PHP, JavaScript, Python), Data Science, Graphic Design, Cyber Security, and Web Development. We also provide specialized training in software like Excel for Data Analysis and tools such as CorelDRAW and Canva.

Do you provide hands-on training?

Yes, all our courses emphasize practical, hands-on training. We believe that students learn best by doing, so you’ll work on real projects and assignments that build your skills in a practical way.

How long do the training programs take?

The duration of each program varies depending on the course. Basic courses may take a few weeks, while advanced programs like Data Science or Networking may last a few months. We offer flexible learning schedules to accommodate your needs.

Can I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course, you will receive a certificate that verifies your participation and the skills you’ve acquired.

How can I register for a course at Yaysib Computer?

You can visit our office to register in person or contact us via phone or email. Our staff will guide you through the process, and we offer flexible payment options for all our courses.

Do you provide consultancy services?

Yes, we offer consultancy in ICT and business management, helping organizations improve their operations with tailored solutions in networking, software development, and other IT needs.
